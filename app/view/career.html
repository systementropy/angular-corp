Career at Network18

Be a part of India’s largest and most admired news network!

Network18 is India's most diversified media conglomerate with news and entertainment brands that touch lives of millions of people.
Despite our size and spread, our Group strives to remain a young, agile, talented and live-wire organisation. We believe we have the soul of a startup, within the body of a media and entertainment colossus. We aim to push boundaries, redefine rules
and put creativity at the centre of everything we do.

Network 18 brings together storytellers, designers, engineers, artists and business managers under one roof united by the hunger to create immersive and bold content and ideas. We take pride in our people, who we believe are the key to realizing the organization’s potential. We continually strive to enable our employees to realize their own goals, by providing opportunities to learn, share and grow.

Life at Network18

  • Exposure – India’s most diversified media conglomerate, Network18 offers an array of work opportunities across digital, broadcasting and publishing space
  • Excitement and Challenge - The dynamism of the organisation coupled with ever-evulving nature of the industry provides abundant exciting and challenging opportunities that can accelerate your learning and growth
  • Empowerment and Entrepreneurial Innovation - At Network18, you get the freedom to take your own decisions and the responsibility to grow through innovation and experimentation
  • Enrichment and learning – Network18 offers customized learning and development initiatives, focused leadership development training and exposure to multi-functional projects. One gets the opportunity to work with some of India’s most credible and celebrated personalities and the chance to learn from them
  • Exuberance - Network18 is young at heart and this is reflected not just in the speed of thinking and execution, but also in its way of life – which values passion, a ‘will-do’ attitude, hunger for success, fun and liveliness.
