Career at Network18

Be a part of India’s largest and most admired news network!

Network18 is India's most diversified media conglomerate with news and entertainment brands that touch lives of millions of people.
Despite our size and spread, our Group strives to remain a young, agile and live-wire organisation. We believe we have the soul of a startup, within the body of a media and entertainment colossus. We aim to push boundaries, redefine rules
and put creativity at the centre of everything we do.

Network 18 brings together employees from varied backgrounds under one roof united by the hunger to create immersive content and ideas. We take pride in our people, who we believe are the key to realizing the organization’s potential. We continually strive to enable our employees to realize their own goals, by providing opportunities to learn, share and grow.

Life at Network18

  • Exposure – As India’s most diversified media conglomerate! We offer an array of work opportunities across digital, broadcasting and publishing space.

  • Excitement and Challenge - The dynamism of the organisation, coupled with the ever-evolving nature of the industry, provides abundant exciting and challenging work opportunities, that will accelerate your learning curve and growth.

  • Empowerment and Entrepreneurial Innovation - At Network18, you get the freedom to innovate, experiment and take your own decisions.

  • Enrichment and learning – We provide multiple opportunities to further the professional growth of our employees through a combination of customized learning and development initiatives and multi-functional projects.

  • Exuberance - We are young at heart and this is reflected not just in our speed of thinking and execution, but also in our way of life – which values passion, a ‘will-do’ attitude, hunger for success, fun and liveliness.
