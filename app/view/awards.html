News18 Rising India Summit

News18 Rising India Summit, a marquee thought leadership initiative of News18, is our enterprise to imagine a collective future to greatness – a showcase of our country’s rising stature in the world and its determination to embrace all the opportunities for a better future. The summit brings together thinkers and scholars from around the world, global statesmen, high achievers in every field of endeavor and, above all, well-wishers of India. CNN-NEWS18 Indian of the Year

The year 2006 saw the introduction of the CNN-NEWS18 Indian of the Year. The initiative recognizes the Indian(s) whose contribution to the country in a calendar year has strengthened the foundation of our society and has helped build Brand India in the process. Having seen 11 editions since its inception, the CNN-NEWS18 Indian of the Year has cemented its position as the biggest awards in the domain of News television and an institution of impeccable credibility.
Over the years, the Indian of the Year award recipients have included the likes of PM Narendra Modi, Indian Cricket Captain Viran Kohli, The Indian Women’s cricket team, NGO Stop Acid Attacks, Chess Grandmaster Vishwanathan Anand, A R Rahman, Former PM Manmohan Singh, The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), Nobel Laureate Kailash Satyarthi, MC Mary Kom, Deepika Padukone, N Chandrasekaran and Arvind Kejriwal.

Chaupal

News18 India is constantly innovating with new shows and formats to create a distinct identity in the general news category. True to its tagline ‘Danke Ki Chot Par’, News18 India practices hard-hitting journalism that promotes constructive criticism and promises positive change. With the same objective in mind, it launched ‘Chaupal’, a daylong conclave for newsmakers, visionaries, youth leaders, decision-makers, top entertainers and sport stars to come together and discuss ideas that unite us and debate issues that divide us. The conclave sees participation of notable dignitaries who share their ideas and views on how to make India a stronger and an evolved society. It is a Socio-political conclave with Ministers, Decision makers, Celebrities, Artists and Educationists discussing & debating about Politics, Economy, Sports, Bollywood and other issues of current importance.

Budget Verdict

CNBC-TV18 has always taken the lead in covering the biggest news developments and assessing their business and economic impact on the lives of Indian citizens. In a constant endeavor to bring innovative formats to its viewers, CNBC-TV18 started The Budget Verdict in 2016. Forbes India Tycoons of Tomorrow

Over the years, apart from chronicling their journey by way of articles, Forbes India seeks to play a catalytic role in identifying and recognizing outstanding young innovators and entrepreneurs. Instituting the Forbes India Tycoons of Tomorrow event is one big step in that direction.

These icons of the future are flag-bearers of family-managed businesses, or first-generation ones ascending in pursuit of securing their own flag-poles amid a highly competitive room at the top. They have displayed the dynamism to revitalize an existing enterprise or created something new, and, in the process, redefined the rules of business and prepared their industry for a whole new vector of growth. The Forbes India Tycoons of Tomorrow listees are felicitated at a sit-down dinner event attended by some of the finest names of Indian Inc. The glittering evening sees insightful talks, panel discussions, and informal conversations covering important contemporary themes.

News18 Mission Paani

Taking the first step towards solving the water crisis, Harpic - News18 Mission Paani is an initiative towards saving water and sustaining it for the generations to come. The aim is to change attitudes and behaviour to improve water use efficiency. Let’s save more water, together. Inspired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s appeal during his Independence Day address to come together for the critical cause of water conservation with ‘Jal Andolan’, Network18, India’s most diversified media conglomerate, along with Amitabh Bachchan, the campaign ambassador and Reckitt Benckiser world’s leading Hygiene Home products company with its premium brand Harpic have announced a partnership for #MissionPaani, which will be a nation-wide campaign.
